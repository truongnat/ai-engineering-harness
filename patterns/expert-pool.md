# Expert Pool

## When To Use

Use this when different specialists can answer targeted questions or review different dimensions of a problem.

## When Not To Use

Do not use it when one operator already has enough context to decide, or when no one is assigned to synthesize conflicting input.

## Agent Team Shape

Multiple specialists plus one decision owner.

## Information Flow

Each specialist answers a narrow question. The decision owner compares the answers and records the final direction in the relevant artifact.

## Strengths

- fast access to multiple perspectives
- good for architecture, testing, and review tradeoffs

## Risks

- conflicting advice
- analysis without synthesis

## Example Use Case

One reviewer checks correctness, one checks testing strategy, and one checks documentation quality before shipping a large change.

## Verification Strategy

The decision owner verifies that expert advice was incorporated into the plan, review, or verification artifacts rather than left implicit.
